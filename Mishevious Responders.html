3 Identifying Mischievous Responders

3.1 Available Items

What items and methods are available to us in determining mischievous responders. First there are two types of “problematic” responders we will be examining:

  1. Straight-lining responders: Trying to complete the survey as fast as possible, diluting the data quality.
  2. Mischievous responders: Impersonating or intentionally misrepresenting their response as someone they are not.

Show the code

  filter(!(RespondentID %in% rids_to_remove))
